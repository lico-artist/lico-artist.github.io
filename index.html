🌌 Mysteries & Myths

🌕 Werewolves: Legends say some humans become wolves under the full moon. Though just a myth, it reflects humanity’s wild side.

🐺 Lone Wolves: Often misunderstood — sometimes it’s survival, sometimes a test of strength.

🔮 Wolves and the Moon: They don’t howl *at* the moon — they howl to talk!

🤯 Fun Facts

  • Wolves can run up to 60 km/h
  • A wolf’s jaw strength is 1500 PSI
  • They mate for life 💞
  • Pups are born blind and deaf
  • They communicate using body language and scent

🦴 Hunting Styles

Wolves are tactical hunters. They use team strategies, flank prey, and always test herds for weak targets before the chase begins.

🔍 Truths vs Myths

  • Myth: Wolves are bloodthirsty killers
    Truth: Wolves avoid humans and hunt only when needed.
  • Myth: Only alphas can mate
    Truth: Alphas usually do, but it’s not a hard rule.
  • Myth: Wolves howl at the moon
    Truth: They howl to locate the pack or alert danger.
